The Most Holy Council Ecumenical On Non-Human Rights of THe High Church of Messiah-as-Emperox

Let it be known to the Sector of Acheron Rho, the investigation of our most Holy Council has reached its conclusions and has found the following:

The Panel on the Social Impact of the Deliverance of Rights unto Aliens has concluded that the impact upon the Sector of the extension of human rights to the lower rungs of the Holy Chain of Being would be of such distruptive influence that any theoretical benefit is quickly overcome.

The Panel on the Material Value of Alien Labour has concluded that, although their theological implications are set in Scripture, more research must be conducted into the utilization of Alien labor to offset the loss of the synthetic workforce.

The Panel on the Intellectual Competency of Aliens has concluded its exhaustive survey of the intellectual capacity of various species of alien. Its data is still being analyzed by Lectorate scientists, but initial analysis indicates no significant deviation from expected baselines.

The Board on the Viability of Alien Hybridization has concluded its initial investigation into the area of Alien Hybridization. It's conclusion is that, while possible, such activity is so repugnant to the sensibilities of any loyal Imperial Citizen as to render the question itself moot.

The Virtue Measurement and Categorisation Committee has concluded its survey of alien Virtue. While there is limited evidence that an Alien can imitate the Virtue of Humanity, there is no evidence, and indeed it appals the Soul of the Virtuous Citizen, to suggest that such Aliens can generate Virtue of their own accord.

The Committee on the Elimination of Aliens from the Chain of Being has concluded that while there are arguments for the elimination of the Alien and Synthetic from the Chain of Being, their exclusion would present certain theological problems. The High Church thanks them and pledges to continue their work.

The Advocacy for Aliens as Doctrinal Allegory, while presenting interesting arguments, has ultimately been forced to conclude that the existence of Aliens is a physical reality, not simply an allegorical one.

The High Exarch thanks the above Committees and Organizations for their selfless efforts on behalf of the Empire and the High Church. Their investigative notes and data will be stored in the central repository on Andophael and will be analyzed by the Holy Lectorate for generations to come.

The High Exarch has delivered the Holy Charter to the constituent council members, and thanks them for their service.

Signed,

Muneer al-Niazi, High Exarch of The High Church of Messiah-as-Emperox
